<div class="step">

Heat the oven to 350°F. Grease and flour a 9×5-inch loaf pan.

</div>

<div class="step">

Whisk the [[large egg for the bread]], [[light brown sugar, packed]], [[granulated sugar for the bread]], [[melted coconut oil]], [[sour cream or Greek yogurt]], and [[vanilla extract]]. Stir in the [[ripe bananas, mashed]].

</div>

<div class="step">

Fold in the [[all-purpose flour for the bread]], [[baking powder]], [[baking soda]], and [[salt]] just until combined. Spread about two-thirds of the batter in the prepared pan.

</div>

<div class="step">

Whisk the [[large egg for the filling]], [[brick-style cream cheese, softened]], [[granulated sugar for the filling]], and [[all-purpose flour for the filling]] until smooth.

</div>

<div class="step">

Spread the cream cheese mixture over the batter, then gently cover it with the remaining banana batter.

</div>

<div class="step">

Bake for 48 to 50 minutes, until the loaf is golden and set. Cool in the pan for 15 minutes, then turn out onto a rack and cool completely before slicing.

</div>
