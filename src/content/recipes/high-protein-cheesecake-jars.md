<div class="step">

Blend the [[cottage cheese]] in a food processor for 1 to 2 minutes, until completely smooth. Add the [[maple syrup]] and blend again.

</div>

<div class="step">

Fold the whipped cottage cheese into the [[plain Greek yogurt]] until creamy and uniform.

</div>

<div class="step">

Divide the [[graham crackers, crushed]], cheesecake mixture, and [[mixed berries]] among four jars in alternating layers. Serve immediately or refrigerate until needed.

</div>
