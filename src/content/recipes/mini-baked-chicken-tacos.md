<div class="step">

Heat the oven to 425°F. Arrange the [[street taco corn tortillas]] on one or two sheet pans and brush both sides lightly with the [[olive oil]].

</div>

<div class="step">

Place a heaping tablespoon of [[cooked seasoned chicken breast, shredded]] and a heaping tablespoon of [[Colby Jack cheese, shredded]] on each tortilla.

</div>

<div class="step">

Bake for 2 minutes to soften the tortillas and melt the cheese. Carefully fold each tortilla in half and press gently, then bake for another 12 to 15 minutes until crisp.

</div>

<div class="step">

Serve with the [[sour cream]], [[salsa]], and [[fresh cilantro, chopped]].

</div>
