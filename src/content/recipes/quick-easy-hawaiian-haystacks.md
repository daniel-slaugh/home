<div class="step">

Whisk the [[cream of chicken soup with herbs]], [[sour cream]], and [[milk]] together in a large skillet over medium heat until smooth and hot.

</div>

<div class="step">

Stir in the [[rotisserie chicken, shredded]] and cook until warmed through.

</div>

<div class="step">

Spoon the chicken gravy over the [[hot cooked rice]]. Build each haystack with [[shredded cheese]], [[pineapple tidbits]], [[sliced black olives]], [[diced tomatoes]], [[sliced green onions]], [[green peas, thawed]], [[crunchy chow mein noodles]], and [[shredded carrots]] as desired.

</div>
