<div class="step">

Heat the oven to 400°F and line a sheet pan with parchment.

</div>

<div class="step">

Spread the [[smoked sausage, sliced]], [[yellow potatoes, cubed]], [[mushrooms, sliced]], [[mixed bell peppers, chopped]], and [[Brussels sprouts, halved]] in a single layer. Drizzle with the [[olive oil]].

</div>

<div class="step">

Mix the [[garlic powder]], [[dried oregano]], [[paprika]], [[red pepper flakes]], [[kosher salt]], and [[black pepper]], then sprinkle over the pan and toss well.

</div>

<div class="step">

Roast for 15 minutes, toss, and roast for another 10 minutes, until the vegetables are tender and the sausage edges are crisp. Finish with the [[fresh parsley, minced]].

</div>
