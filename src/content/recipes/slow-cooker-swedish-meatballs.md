<div class="step">

Lightly grease the slow cooker insert and add the [[frozen Italian-style or homestyle meatballs]].

</div>

<div class="step">

Whisk together the [[reduced-sodium beef broth]], [[cream of mushroom soup]], [[brown gravy mix]], [[garlic powder]], and [[water]]. Pour the mixture over the meatballs.

</div>

<div class="step">

Cover and cook on Low for 4 to 5 hours, until the meatballs are hot throughout.

</div>

<div class="step">

Stir in the [[evaporated milk]], cover again, and cook for 15 to 30 minutes. Serve promptly over [[cooked egg noodles or rice, for serving]].

</div>
